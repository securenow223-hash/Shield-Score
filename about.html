About Shield & Score

Behind the benchmark data: our commitment to uncompromised digital safety research.

🔬 The Blueprint Behind Our Audits

Founded on the principle that consumers deserve empirical truth rather than promotional echo chambers, Shield & Score operates as an independent cybersecurity testing portal. In an industry crowded with sponsored roundups and vague performance claims, our group of independent researchers subjects consumer antivirus suites to rigorous, reproducible stress tests.

We do not rely on theoretical simulations or recycled press releases. Every software package we analyze is installed on standardized hardware environments and tested against live threat vectors to measure true quarantine efficacy and resource consumption patterns.

⚙️ Core Pillars of Our Research Framework

📊 Empirical Precision

Our metrics are gathered through direct diagnostic tracing, clocking reaction speeds down to the millisecond under active payload executions.

🛡️ Absolute Objectivity

We maintain strict editorial independence. No software vendor can purchase, influence, or alter our audit findings or comparative scores.

🔄 Continuous Evolution

Cyber threats evolve daily; therefore, our testing methodologies and software benchmarks are updated continuously throughout 2026.

Structural Transparency Notice: Shield & Score is a dedicated consumer research publisher. While we provide detailed evaluations of major platforms like Norton, Bitdefender, McAfee, and Avast, we maintain zero structural ownership or equity agreements with any security developer.

📌 Our Commitment to You

Navigating digital protection options should be transparent, data-driven, and completely straightforward. Whether you are safeguarding a personal home workstation or managing multi-device household coverage, our goal is to equip you with the precise technical intelligence needed to make an informed, confident software choice.
