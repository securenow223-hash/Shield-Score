Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

At Shield & Score, accessible from our publication pages, the privacy of our visitors is of paramount importance. This Privacy Policy document outlines the categories of information that are collected and recorded by our platform and how we utilize it.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact our editorial desk via our contact portal.

🔒 Information We Collect

Shield & Score operates as an independent research publisher. We collect information through standard log files when visitors browse our evaluation pages. The information collected by log files includes:

  • Internet Protocol (IP) addresses
  • Browser type and version specifications
  • Internet Service Provider (ISP) details
  • Date and time stamps of access
  • Referring and exit web pages
  • Click metrics related to site navigation

This data is not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. The purpose of the information is for analyzing statistical trends, administering the site, tracking user movement on the website, and gathering demographic intelligence.

🍪 Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any professional digital publisher, Shield & Score uses cookies to store information including visitor preferences and the specific pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. This information is utilized to optimize user experience by customizing our web page content based on browser type and other relevant navigational data.
